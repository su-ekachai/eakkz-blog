Hi, I’m **Ekachai** (Eakkz). Welcome to my unorganized blog!

I’m a data engineer from Thailand who loves working with data, and—most importantly—taking care of my **7 cats**. 🐈

To be honest, I’m someone who gets bored pretty easily. One day I might be deep into **Data Engineering** and **Python**, and the next I might be obsessing over **Finance**, **Trading strategies**, or just the latest gadget I bought.

**A quick heads-up:** I’m not really a writer, so don't expect long, poetic essays. Since I am from Thailand, you might find my content in both **Thai** and **English**.

So, this blog is going to be a bit of a mix. You’ll find:

*   Technical guides and coding tips (mostly so I don't forget them myself).
*   Notes on data and technology.
*   Musings on finance and trading.
*   And probably some cat photos. 😸

This is just a space for me to document whatever I’m currently interested in. Whether you’re here for the code or just curious, I hope you find something useful!

Thanks for stopping by.

> **Full transparency:** I sometimes use AI tools to help polish my writing. The ideas and experiences are 100% mine, but the smooth grammar? That might be the AI helping me out.
